Privacy Policy for Just Lift

Effective date: June 10, 2026  ·  Developer: Adam Nix

Just Lift stores all of your data locally on your device.

The app does not automatically send your data to any server. It contains no analytics, no advertising, no account or login system, and no third-party data sharing. The only way data leaves your device is if you choose to export it yourself.

Information we store

Just Lift stores the following information locally on your device so the app can function:

  • Workout logs — exercise name, weight, reps, sets, optional notes, and the date and time of each workout
  • Workout plans — the training days and exercises you build in the Plan tab
  • Custom exercises — any exercises you add that are not in the built-in list
  • Body weight entries — the weight and date of each entry you log
  • App preferences — small settings such as whether you have seen the welcome screen

The app does not collect your name, email address, location, contacts, photos, or any other personal information.

How we use information

Your data is used only to make the app work on your device:

  • Logging and viewing your workout history
  • Building and editing workout plans
  • Showing progress charts, statistics, streaks, and personal records
  • Tracking body weight over time
  • Creating backups when you choose to export, and restoring them when you choose to import

Your data is never used for advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than the app's own features.

Sharing and transmission

  • The app does not automatically transmit your data off the device.
  • The app does not share your data with third parties.
  • The app does not use analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting services that send user data anywhere.
  • If you use the Export feature, the app creates a backup file (JSON or plain text) on your device and opens the standard system share/save dialog. You choose where that file goes. The app itself does not upload it anywhere. Once you send an export to another app or service, that service's own privacy policy applies.

Data storage and security

All data is stored on your device in the app's private local storage (IndexedDB and localStorage inside the app's own sandboxed storage area). It stays on your device unless you manually export it. Because there are no servers and no accounts, there is no copy of your data anywhere else.

Deleting your data

You are in full control of your data and can delete it at any time:

  • In the app — you can delete individual workout logs, body weight entries, custom exercises, and plan days.
  • On your device — uninstalling the app, or clearing the app's storage in your device settings, permanently removes all locally stored data.

Because we never receive your data, there is nothing for us to delete on a server. If you need help deleting your data, you can contact us at the email address below.

Children's privacy

Just Lift is intended for a general audience. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13, and no data ever leaves the device unless the user exports it. We do not request, store, or transmit any information that could identify a child.
